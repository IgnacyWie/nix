# Intel-Only Apps Inventory

## Reviewed Baseline

No Intel-only application is currently required by the v1 Workstation baseline.
Rosetta 2 remains an install-if-needed manual step.

## Validation

During restore or app inventory review, record any Intel-only app here before
making Rosetta 2 a required baseline. Include the app name, why it is required,
and whether an Apple Silicon replacement exists.
